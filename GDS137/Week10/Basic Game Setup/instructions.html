Welcome to the Instructions to Cave Escape!

You must use various skills to platform to each area of the cave


You will have to collect the blue orbs in each area to continue

Once you collect the final blue orb you will escape!

Area 1: Treacherous Platforms

Stand on the platforms and move with them to reach the goal!

Area 2: Currents of Chaos

Use the power of the blue wind platform to defy gravity!

Area 3: Perilous Portals

Touch the yellow portal if you dare to reach the goal.

Area 4: Detrimental Elemental Platforms

Use the buttons to change color, if you aren't the same color as the platform

you are currently on, you will die. Period.

Area 5: Keys to Freedom

A locked door stands between you and freedom

Collect all 4 yellow orbs to unlock the door and win!



Basic Controls

W - Jump

A - Left

D - right
